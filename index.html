About SRM KTR Campus

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) Kattankulathur (KTR) Campus is one of the largest and most prestigious educational institutions in India. Spread over 250 acres, the campus boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, world-class laboratories, and a vibrant research environment.

Advanced Labs and Facilities

SRM KTR Campus is home to some of the most advanced laboratories in the country, providing students with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technology. Key highlights include:

  • Mac Lab: Equipped with the latest Apple devices, supporting projects in design, media, and software development.
  • AI & Robotics Lab: Fostering innovation in artificial intelligence and robotics with state-of-the-art tools and systems.
  • High-Performance Computing Lab: Featuring powerful computing clusters for research in data science, machine learning, and more.
  • Virtualization & Cloud Lab: Enabling students to work on cloud computing, virtualization technologies, and network security.
  • Dedicated labs for Packet Tracer, Linux, and advanced networking studies.

Our labs are constantly upgraded to keep pace with the latest industry trends, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for the future.

Campus Life at SRM KTR

SRM KTR offers a vibrant campus life with a perfect blend of academics, research, and extracurricular activities. The campus hosts numerous technical fests, cultural events, and sports competitions, providing students with ample opportunities to showcase their talents and skills.
